Privacy Policy

Learn how Egyptian American Cultural Center collects, uses, and protects personal information submitted through our website and services.

On this page

Overview Information Collection When We Collect Why We Use It How We Use It Information Security

Egyptian American Cultural Center is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This statement explains how we collect, use and protect your information when you visit our website and use our courses and services.

Privacy Policy

You can visit our website without disclosing any personally identifiable information about yourself. If you do submit personal information by ordering our services, you can be assured that we will use your personal information only to support your continuing relationship with Egyptian American Cultural Center.

This policy, together with our terms of use, sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed by us.

In order to register and receive or use the services on our website, you will be required to submit some personal information, such as your name, your phone number and email address. We ensure that we keep your personal data safe and secure. We will not share your personal information with anybody else without your knowledge unless we are required by law to do so.

Personal Information Collection

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Information that you provide by submitting your application when enrolling on a course or applying for a job.
  • Identifying information when you contact us for assistance.
  • If you email us, we may keep a record of that correspondence.
  • Feedback forms used for quality assurance or research purposes. These forms are optional.
  • Details of transactions you carry out through our site and of the fulfilment of your orders.
  • Details of your visits to our site including, but not limited to, number of visits, time spent on the site, traffic data, location data, and other communication data.
  • Personal information such as name, address, email and Student ID number.

When Do We Collect Information?

We endeavour to collect and use your personal information only with your knowledge and consent, typically when you:

  • Order and subsequently use services.
  • Make customer enquiries.
  • Register for information or other services.
  • Request product information.
  • Submit a job application or send a CV.
  • Graduate from our Center.
  • Work with us.
  • Respond to communications from us, such as questionnaires or surveys.

Why Do We Use Your Information?

We collect information about you for two reasons: firstly, to process your order, and secondly, to provide you with the best possible service. The type of personal information we may collect could include your name and postal address, date of birth, gender, telephone and email address.

We do not keep a record of your credit or debit card information. If you choose to provide us with personal information, it will be used in support of the intended purposes stated at the time it was collected, and subject to any preferences indicated by you.

How Will We Use Your Information?

We may use your information for a number of purposes, including processing your enrolment, managing, administering and delivering our qualifications or information requested by you, and responding to complaints or enquiries.

Information Security

If you have any enquiry about Egyptian American Cultural Center's data protection and privacy policy or practices, please send an email to info_eacc@eacc-egy.com.
