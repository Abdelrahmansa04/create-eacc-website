Why MyVoice

A purposeful camp.
A future-ready journey.

MyVoice is a premium summer experience designed to help children discover their voice, build real confidence, and grow through English communication, creativity, technology, leadership, and cultural exploration.

01
Personalized from the very first step
Before camp begins, every child completes an English placement test plus a personality and interests assessment with Mind Space, allowing the experience to match their level, strengths, and natural curiosity.
Mind Space partnership
02
British-inspired English learning
The English track is built on Oxford Discover, published by Oxford University Press and aligned with CEFR. Children practise listening and speaking through meaningful communication instead of memorised grammar routines.
Oxford Discover · CEFR-aligned
03
Four pillars for whole-child growth
Communicate, innovate, lead, and connect. Each pillar strengthens a different part of the child, helping them leave with stronger confidence, practical skills, and a clearer sense of identity.
4 core pillars
04
A final showcase with real pride
The journey ends with the MyVoice Showcase, where children present their final projects to families and educators and are celebrated for creativity, teamwork, leadership, and innovation.
Graduation event

MyVoice Parenting Club

A stronger child starts with a supported family.

Because growth continues at home, the MyVoice Parenting Club offers specialist-led workshops by Mind Space to help families support confidence, bilingual development, emotional wellbeing, and healthy technology habits.

🧠
Confidence and self-expression at home
Practical guidance for helping your child speak, share feelings, and express ideas with calm confidence.
🌐
Raising bilingual children
Simple, realistic routines that support English development while protecting Arabic identity and family communication.
📱
Parenting in the AI era
A balanced look at AI, screens, and digital learning so parents can guide children with confidence instead of fear.
👦
Understanding Generation Alpha
A clear view of today's children, how they think, what motivates them, and how they learn best.
