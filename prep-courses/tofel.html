Why TOEFL?

A global benchmark for English proficiency.

The Test of English as a Foreign Language (TOEFL) assesses English proficiency in Reading, Writing, Listening, and Speaking. Whether you are planning to study at a prestigious university, pursue a career in an English-speaking country, or achieve your migration goals, a high TOEFL score opens doors to global opportunities.

At EACC, we offer specialized courses to help you succeed in Academic TOEFL for university admissions and Home Edition TOEFL for remote testing.
